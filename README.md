# Ignavia
A Hex Casting addon for April Fools.  

Ignavia is the latin translation of the Greek goddess of sloth, idleness, and laziness: Aergia.  
Basically the polar opposite of Hermes, whom is associated with effort and activity while Aergia is the literal goddess of no effort. You can rent a disagreement at idontcareaergiaisacoolnameokay.com lmao
